Wedding Details

Ceremony Balboa Park 8/18/2018

Our wedding ceremony will be held at the Botanical Lawns (in front of the Botanical Building) at Balboa Park. The ceremony will take place at 4:30 PM.

Reception MOPA Dinner, Drinks, & Dancing

After the ceremony, there will be a cocktail/appetizer hour at the lawn next to the ceremony while the reception is set up. When the reception is ready, Kate's family will direct you on a short walk to the Museum of Photographic Arts (MOPA) for dinner, drinks, and dancing.

The map below will show you the locations of parking, the ceremony, and the reception. A more detailed map has been included in your invitation.

Questions And Answers

Where do I park?

You can park for free in any of Balboa Park's lots. The nearest lots are the Organ Pavilion parking lot or the South Caoursel parking lot.

What's for dinner?

Dinner will feature main courses of salmon and tri-tip beef, along with sides. We will also have finger foods/appetizers and drinks offered between the reception and the ceremony.

Will there be alcohol?

Alcohol will be provided by the bride and groom. There will be an open bar.

How far is the ceremony from the reception? Will I have to move my car or drive?

The ceremony is located one-tenth of a mile from the reception. You should not move your car.

I have another question..

Please reach out to Aries or Kate directly.
